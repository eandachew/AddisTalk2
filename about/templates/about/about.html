About AddisTalk

AddisTalk is a community-driven platform created to share ideas, stories, and discussions that matter.

This project was built using Django as part of a full-stack development journey, with a focus on clean design, scalability, and real-world functionality.

Our goal is to create a space where users can read, comment, and engage in meaningful conversations.
